How we work

Values we actually live by

We're building something hard. These are the principles that guide how we work together every day.

🔭

Think in Systems

We zoom out before we zoom in. Understanding the full picture leads to better decisions than optimizing in isolation.

⚡

Bias for Action

We ship, learn, and iterate. A working prototype beats a perfect plan. We trust each other to move fast and own the outcome.

🤝

Radical Transparency

We share context widely, give direct feedback, and have hard conversations early. No politics, no silos.

🧩

Customer Obsession

Our customers deploy AI in high-stakes environments. We treat their trust as sacred — every decision starts with their needs.

📈

Grow Relentlessly

We hire curious people and invest in making them exceptional. We expect everyone to be better six months from now.

🌍

Build for Everyone

AI will shape the world. We build equitably and responsibly, with teams that reflect the diversity of the people we serve.
